About Desk Haus

Desk Haus is an independent review site focused on home-office and desk setup gear — standing desks, ergonomic chairs, monitors, mechanical keyboards, and webcams. We research the products that matter for building a workspace you'll actually enjoy, cut through the marketing, and recommend the gear that earns its place on a desk.

Affiliate Disclosure

Desk Haus is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. When you click a link to Amazon on this site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site running and our reviews free to read. It never changes the price you pay, and it never influences which products we recommend.

How We Test

Our recommendations are built on a mix of hands-on use, deep research, and real-world ownership signals. For each category we:

  • Cross-check expert lab reviews, ergonomic guidance, and manufacturer specifications.
  • Read through thousands of verified long-term owner reviews to spot reliability and durability patterns.
  • Rank every product against its direct competitors at the same price tier, not in isolation.
  • Prioritize comfort, build quality, and day-after-day reliability over flashy spec sheets.
  • Revisit and update our picks as new models launch and prices shift.

If a product stops being the best choice, we change our recommendation. Our goal is to be the guide we wish we'd had when building our own setups.

Privacy Policy

We keep things simple. Desk Haus does not sell your personal information. You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time.
